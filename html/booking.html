About Lujo Juno

Lujo Juno has for several generations, under the auspices of the Lujo Juno family, been known for delicious food and a pleasant atmosphere - as well as a beautiful venue with a lovely view of the sea.

Our restaurant

We run like a normal restaurant on the weekdays and rent out our large venue. Our venue has space for 100 people, with an outdoor area and an indoor area. You can book our venue with or without a restaurant.
