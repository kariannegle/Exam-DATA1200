Knut Jørgen Vie expresses that changes in society's values ​​can affect the development of various technologies, and he highlights social media as an example. In the video, Vie says "this technology has had a significant impact on the social life for many people, but the values ​​these people hold are finding their way back into technology" (Oslomet, 2018-2020, 5:49).


Social media and chat services have evolved to become more complex and people who use these platforms have had their values ​​shaped. This is done by actively engaging with other people, for example family, friends or other students. As a society, we have thus defined social media as the "informal way" to seek out and keep in touch.


The development of social media and chat services has influenced the social norms in today's society. This is because we humans today communicate almost exclusively through social media. We are positive about the development since we as students can easily keep in touch with other students today.
